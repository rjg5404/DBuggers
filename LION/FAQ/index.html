General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Lion is On is required.

Do I have to be a member of the Penn State community?

Yes. You are required to have a Penn State email address in order to sign up. We want this market place to be exclusive to the Penn State community.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All prices for everything including each seller's or buyer's account balance are in USD. However, we are looking to add Bitcoin as an accepted form of payment at some point in the future.
Sellers

Who cen sell items?

Anybody! You just need to have a valid Penn State University email address.

I want to sell my items - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account
  • Go to the Sell page and upload a picture of your item along with a description and some other required information
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about a couple hours.

How much do I get from each sale?

100%! We are a non-profit auction website.

Why sell my items here?

There are a number of reasons why you should join us:
  • We work for free.
  • Fast response/approval times. Many sites take weeks to process an item but we are constantly working around the clock for you.
  • It's all Penn State members so everyone on this website lives in the area.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment. Also Venmo is an option.

When do I get paid?

Whenever your buyer pays you.
